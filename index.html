Why Vitalis exists

Games about the machinery behind the dream.

Vitalis Interactive began with a simple question: could the systems behind wealth, ownership, and legacy become a game?

We build simulations for players who enjoy understanding how things actually work—not just watching a number climb. Our games are grounded in recognizable decisions, meaningful tradeoffs, and consequences that unfold over time.

Independent. Systems-driven. Built to grow.

Family Office Simulator

Wealth is temporary. Institutions endure.

Build and manage a real multi-entity family office. Acquire income-producing property, structure debt, hire staff, form trusts, govern the institution, and prepare the next generation to inherit more than a balance sheet.

  • Build a team and headquarters
  • Govern, protect, and transfer wealth
